# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Voyalier welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, ability, nationality, or travel history.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful impact directly.
- Welcome questions and explain unfamiliar concepts without condescension.
- Respect privacy. Never post another person's itinerary, travel documents, credentials, or identifying data.
- Keep product and technical disagreement focused on evidence and tradeoffs.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate exposure of private information, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through the repository's private security-reporting channel. Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors when necessary to protect the community.

This policy is based on the Contributor Covenant 2.1 and may be expanded as the community grows.
