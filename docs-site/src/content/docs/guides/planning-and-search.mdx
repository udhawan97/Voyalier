---
title: Saved places, packing, and manual plans
description: Turn local suggestions and half-made ideas into traveler-owned plans without calling them bookings.
---

Voyalier keeps **confirmed evidence** and **your own plan** in separate lanes. A
saved recommendation, checklist item, or typed museum visit is useful—but it is
not a reservation and never changes logistics readiness.

## Save a place

Download a city pack, set the food/culture/nature/nightlife/shopping weights,
choose **Save interests** when you want those weights to persist, and then choose
**Get recommendations**. Select **Save place** to keep a result's name,
coordinates, reasons, source, and license, then add your own private notes.

Removing the source pack does not remove the shortlist. Voyalier marks the pack
as unavailable while preserving the provenance snapshot. **Add to plan**
prefills the same traveler-authored activity form with the saved place and its
coordinates; review the form and submit it before anything enters the plan.

## Build the packing checklist

Packing suggestions are derived from the trip and stored weather evidence. They
never enter the checklist silently: choose **Add** for a suggestion you want, or
type a custom item. Check, uncheck, or remove items as the trip changes. Refreshing
weather does not rewrite traveler-owned checklist state. Accepted and custom
items can also be renamed; unchecked items stay above completed ones. The custom
**Add** action stays disabled until you enter an item, so an empty click cannot
look like a lost change.

## Add an activity, rail leg, or transfer

Enter a title and, optionally, a location, floating local start/end time, and
private notes. Items can be edited or removed. If two timed items literally
overlap, Voyalier shows a planning notice; the notice does not become a booking
conflict or affect readiness. A name is required: submitting a blank form shows
the requirement beside the field and moves keyboard focus there.

Scheduled manual items can appear in Today, the printable brief, and `.ics`
calendar export. The export projection has fields only for title, kind,
location, and times, so private notes cannot enter it.

## Search the workspace

Use **Search workspace** from the top bar to search every trip's imported
documents, confirmed facts, trip notes, saved places, and manual plans. Each
result names its trip and source kind. Pending extraction candidates remain out
of search until you review them. Search is a bounded local scan: no query or
index leaves the device.

## Return after a reload

While a trip is open, reloading returns to that trip and preserves a selected
Plan, Prepare, Discover, or AI section. Voyalier keeps only the active trip id in
browser session storage for this re-entry hint—never the trip's content,
evidence, notes, or search text. Choosing **All trips** clears the hint and any
stale section link.

## Language and source transparency

Settings includes **Language** (System, English, or Español) and **Data sources &
licenses**. A language change applies immediately and stays in local browser
storage; every application message has both English and Spanish copy. The
topbar and Settings theme controls share one Light/System/Dark preference, so
changing either updates both immediately. The
20-entry source register groups built-in, consent-fetched, offline-download, and
optional-AI inputs and names each authority and license boundary. It does not
merge different sources into a trust score.
